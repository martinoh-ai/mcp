Nuestra Historia

A Masia Reborn

Can Pares traces its origins to the 16th century, when it served as a working agricultural estate in the heart of Catalonia. The traditional masia farmhouse, with its distinctive stone walls and terracotta tiles, witnessed centuries of Catalan rural life.

Over the generations, the estate evolved—from farming to wine production, through periods of prosperity and quiet dormancy. The original structures remained, their thick walls holding stories of harvests, festivals, and family gatherings.

In recent years, the property found new guardians: a family passionate about preserving its heritage while breathing new life into its spaces. The restoration balanced authenticity with comfort, honoring the past while embracing the present.
