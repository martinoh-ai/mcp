A Natural Treasure

Parc Natural del Garraf

Just minutes from Can Pares lies one of Catalonia's most spectacular natural parks. The Garraf massif is a karst landscape of white limestone, sculpted over millennia by wind and water into dramatic cliffs, deep valleys, and over 400 caves.

The landscape feels almost desert-like in places‚Äîdry Mediterranean scrubland dotted with the distinctive fan palm (Europe's only native palm). Then suddenly, you turn a corner to find yourself among holm oaks, with views stretching to the sea. It's a land of contrasts, of ancient Iberian settlements and medieval hermitages, of Bonelli's eagles soaring overhead.

Best Seasons

Spring (March-May) and autumn (Sept-Nov) offer perfect hiking weather. Summer can be extremely hot‚Äîstart early if hiking then. Winter is mild but occasional rain.

What to Bring

Plenty of water (no sources on trails), sun protection, sturdy shoes, and binoculars for bird watching. Trail maps available at La Pleta visitor center.

üêï

Dogs Welcome

Dogs are allowed in the park but must be kept on a leash at all times. Some areas may be restricted during bird nesting season (spring).

Getting There

Most trailheads are 10-20 minutes from Can Pares by car. Parking available at La Pleta, Olivella, and Garraf beach. Some trails accessible by train.
