Arrival

Getting Here

Can Pares is easily accessible by car, with private parking within the gated property. We recommend renting a car to fully explore the region, though taxis and transfers can be arranged.

By Car

Take the C-32 motorway towards Sitges, exit at Sant Pere de Ribes, follow signs to Torre del Veguer. Private parking on property.

Airport Transfer

We can arrange private transfers from Barcelona El Prat Airport (approximately 25 minutes). Contact us for details.

By Train

Trains run from Barcelona to Sitges (30 min) or Vilanova (40 min). A short taxi ride brings you to Can Pares.
