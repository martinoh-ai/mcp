Experiences

From Penedès wine tours to Mediterranean sailing, discover curated experiences that make your stay unforgettable.

A region made for discovery—and we're your guide.

Can Pares sits at the crossroads of mountains and sea, vineyards and beaches. Within 30 minutes, you can be tasting cava in a 500-year-old cellar, sailing the Costa del Garraf, hiking through ancient forests, or exploring the magic of Montserrat.

We've spent years curating the best local experiences—from family wineries to private sailing charters, from cooking classes with local chefs to secret hiking trails. Every recommendation comes from personal experience.

Your Personal Concierge Tell us what you love, and we'll craft the perfect itinerary. From booking reservations to arranging private tours, we handle every detail so you can simply enjoy.
