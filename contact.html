Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What are check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is from 4:00 PM and check-out is by 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request—just ask us!

Is parking available?

Yes! We have private parking within our gated property, free for all guests. There's plenty of space for multiple vehicles.

Can you arrange airport transfers?

Absolutely. We can arrange private transfers from Barcelona El Prat Airport (approximately 25 minutes). Contact us for details and pricing.

Are pets allowed?

We welcome well-behaved pets in certain accommodations. Please let us know in advance so we can make the necessary arrangements.
